A closer step into the future...

The Plate Recognition App is a bachelor's degree final project meant to show how convolutional neural networks can be put at work in order to identify and recognize license plates. Feel free to test the app by uploading a picture or add a plate to the Projec's database. If the license plate of the car picture you uploaded is already in the database you will be presented with a checked icon.
